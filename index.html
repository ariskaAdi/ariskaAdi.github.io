About Us

Oddwain is a rock duo from suburban area of Malang, Indonesia. The band possessed by the souls of Adi Prasetyo (Guitar, Vocal) and Galih Fathurohman (Drum). Oddwain merge plentiful spectrum of alt-rock and pop music into their own works.The name Oddwain was founded by Galih Fathurohman in 2016.

The complete discography of Oddwain displays how broad their music can be. They could go from ice-cold indie-pop music to hot and bold rock outfit. Oddwain is currently under Pops You Good management, a record label initiated by Atlesta. Oddwain consistently put complex abstraction on their lyrics. Oddwain's lyrics can be simply described as multi-layered works. Their cosmic sense lies in the core of the songs, then they layer it with philosophical touch, and wrap it up with their typical wordplay. Oddwain have no grand design for their music as they believed that music is a labour of love.
